About Me

My name is Ishan Jandaur and I recently graduated from California Polytechnic State University - San Luis Obispo. I was admitted as a student studying mechanical engineering despite not having an idea of what the major consisted of exactly. I enjoyed playing with Legos as a kid and figured that it would be something I enjoyed. Over the past four years I have grown to love the versatility and never-ending amount of knowledge I learn in this field. Furthermore, I enjoy being able to work with my hands and watch my imagination amount to tangible products.

I am currently looking for a full-time job as a mechanical engineer and hope to end up in technical program management in the future.

Some of my hobbies include playing basketball, cooking, and collecting vinyl records.
